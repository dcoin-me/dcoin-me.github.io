Emission of coins allows you to earn money

Coins <=> cash

Users pinpoint their location on the map and how much cash they’re ready to exchange for Dcoins. The rate is always 1:1. At this time, max amount for USD is $1’000

New coins

Users willing to exchange cash for Dcoins earn bonus coins. This is a reward for serving as an ATM

Balance

In case coin volume becomes too high or if there is not enough cash for Dcoins, coin volume is automatically decreased. This results in balancing amount of coins to ensure 1:1 exchange rate.
